How does the process work?

To receive a stamp of approval from Verified Binary, trading applications are required to pass 5 rigorous steps.

When the owner of a trading platform decides to file a case for approval with Verified Binary, they must report all claims of performance and submit performance statistics of each individual user.

Verified Binary conducts a thorough review of performance statistics. Statistics are verified so we ensure performance claims adhere to the actual performance of an average user.This evaluation also includes submission of original and notarised broker statements.

Verified Binary evaluates the safety, security and reliability of the platform through rigorous testing of the platform.

All contact information, support lines, and business information is verified by a member of our board.

The risk for the user is thoroughly assessed and the result of this assessment must be explicitly stated when bringing the application to market.

How often are Binary accounts are verified?

Binary accounts are verified every two years.

Stamps of approval are only valid for 2 years and must be submitted to pass through all 5 steps before expiry of approval.

How do I apply to become a board member of the Verified Binary Team?

To become a board member, you must submit your resume and cover letter to our email at careers@verifiedbinary.com

How do I report abuse?

It is an offense to use our seals without permission. If you see any suspicious use of our seals of approval, please report it to us immediately. We take this very seriously and will take swift and aggressive action.
