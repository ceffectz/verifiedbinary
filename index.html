Verified Binary is a third-party regulator of Binary Options Trading systems and applications. Since being founded in 2004, we have been dedicated to protecting the public from unethical and unreliable trading software by providing unbiased and objective evaluations.

Our CEO, Gary Lewis, states, “At Verified Binary, we believe we have a duty to protect our users from unreliable and unverified trading applications. Our stamp of approval is only granted to the very best trading applications available on the market today. For over 10 years, we have been relentless in our pursuit of eliminating unacceptable trading software from market.We always perform our due diligence in every case and we pride ourselves in over a decade of exceptional service.”
